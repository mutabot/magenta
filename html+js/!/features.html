Magenta River Features At glance


Create a text, link, or image post on Google Plus and let the Crosspost Service to distribute your content to multiple target social networks.

The Crosspost Service applies advanced algorithms to reproduce your content in target network as close to original Google Plus post as possible.

Keyword filters allow to set up advanced multi-source to multi-target content replication configurations.

Functionality

Features

  • Post scheduling via configurable "allowed" time-windows
  • Keyword filtering with optional keyword stripping
  • Post popularity filtering
  • Full-resolution image upload *
  • Image albums transfer (were albums are supported)
  • Text formatting as close to source as supported by destination
  • Updated posts auto-update at destination **
  • Links to source for reshared content
  • Custom Taglines / Signatures

Specifications

  • Update period: Adaptive to user activity, 5 to 30 minutes ***
  • User capacity: Unlimited
  • Scheduler frame: 1 week

Fineprint

* Target service may reduce image size and/or quality

** When image is updated in Google Plus previously crossposted image on Facebook is deleted and re-created fresh, all Facebook comments and likes will be lost. Only a few most recent posts are supported by update engine.

*** Adaptive for each users' individual daily activity patterns.

All features and specifications are subject to change without notice.
