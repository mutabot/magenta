Magenta River Pty. Ltd. is established in Sydney, Australia.

The Crosspost Service is an offspring of our photography business.

The service was initially created in mid- 2013 for our personal use. Self-hosting a crosspost was a great help as at that time there were no suitable solutions available to automate posting to multiple social networks.

After initial pilot release's success we have made a descision to establish a proper public service.

In January 2014 the Crosspost Service was released to a monitored and backed-up cloud-based platform.

Our promise is to keep building the Crosspost Service for as long as there will be a demand from our users.


Please support our service by promoting it on your pages!
