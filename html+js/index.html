SECURE

Cookies

Our site uses "cookies". Web browser places cookies on hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about the user. You may choose to set web browser to refuse cookies. If you do so you will not be able to sing-in to the service.

Passwords

We never ask for any of your passwords.

You have to authorize Magenta River to post on your behalf. The authorization is granted via a workflow that guarantees that your personal credentials are not seen by our servers.

Authorization for every social network can always be revoked on each network's site. Once authorization is removed, Magenta River will loose ability to post on your behalf.
